Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • Milk to knead dough
  • Sugar syrup

Recipe

  1. Mix milk powder, flour, and ghee.
  2. Knead into a soft dough using milk.
  3. Shape into small balls.
  4. Deep fry until golden brown.
  5. Soak in sugar syrup for 1 hour before serving.

Barfi

Ingredients

  • 1 cup condensed milk
  • 1 cup khoya
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom powder
  • Chopped nuts for garnish

Recipe

  1. Mix khoya, condensed milk, and sugar in a pan.
  2. Cook on low heat until it thickens.
  3. Add cardamom powder and mix well.
  4. Pour into a greased tray and flatten evenly.
  5. Garnish with chopped nuts, cool and cut into squares.
